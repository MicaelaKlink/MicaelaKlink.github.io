Graphic Design

Logos, Banners and Videos | From concept to creation

Digital design...a newfound medium, or somewhat new to me. In recent years, I shifted my focus from traditional art to digital design due to changes in the market. Despite this shift, I grew fond of graphic design and content creation in general. Unlike traditional art, it's easy to just undo something...Ctrl + Z really being my favorite command. Furthermore, there's so much you can do digitally, such as creating videos...let's just say, the options for creativity are endless.
