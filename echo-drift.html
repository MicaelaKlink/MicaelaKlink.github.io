Project Overview

Background: I decided to take the initiative to challenge myself and step into a new environment. This led me to asking AI, well, ChatGPT in particular to give me a request so I can practice freelancing. And so, they gave me the brief that is listed below.

Brief: "Design a cover for a fictional indie band called Soft Comet. Album name: Echo Drift. The band wants a dreamy, space-themed aesthetic with soft pastel tones."

So, here's my journey below:

Design Journey

Design Process

Going into this project, I was set on adding a figure that is sitting on something. Additionally, I really wanted to add a planet. Going along with these ideas, I decided to add the album cover name as the rings for the planet, making it a union.

After composition, I decided to add blurring layers to make it dreamy but also not too plain. I added a half blur effect on the figure to signify that the figure drifting, hence, linking it to the album's name. I also, used a figure that is leaning to further emphasis this and to connect it to the dreamy vibe. The last details before struggling with the font of the band's name was adding galaxy themed images within my circle and text to make it space-themed.

Project Reflection

Going into this project was really something different and surprisingly not as challenging as I thought it would be. Coming into this project, I never really knew what the indie aesthetic is, and if I'm to be completely honest, I still do not fully grasp the style. Additionally, I never made an actual album cover before besides painting one based off a poem I wrote. Although I feel like digital art gives more creative freedom, comparing to the painting I did, I will admit that at times, I felt like painting this project would have been easier. But I still love the digital solution as it allowed me to play around without making a permanent mistake.
