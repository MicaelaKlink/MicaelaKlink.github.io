Traditional Art

Where it all started...or at least for me. Just different mediums along with ideas and thoughts

Traditional art is where it all began...a scrap of paper and a pen. It's something many of us have strayed from, including myself, yet it remains the foundation of creativity. From doodling in school notebooks to sketching mockups for digital designs, these early practices shaped how we think and create. Below are art pieces I've completed over the years, most of them being conceptual and some based on poems I wrote. Enjoy them and feel free to interpret them however they speak to you.
