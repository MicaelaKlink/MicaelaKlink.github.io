About Me

I recently graduated (2025) with a BCom General degree, majoring in Information Systems and Finance Management at the University of the Western Cape. I specialise in development and design, combining technical problem-solving with creative thinking to build solutions that are functional, visually appealing, and user-focused. I thrive in dynamic environments where every month looks different.

I am driven by curiosity and take initiative on challenges. In 2022, I received a leadership award for my contributions to Covid screening initiatives. In 2025, I led a team to develop a chatbot for external clients that retrieves company data, achieving a 92% grade. Whether integrating hardware or designing a brand identity, I'm passionate about delivering high-quality and functional solutions.

Beyond code and design, I explore interior design, video editing, and dance. You can find my Sims 4 builds and dance content on YouTube and TikTok. I am guided by three principles: Curiosity, Creativity, and Creation with a brand "I Don't EvenS Know™", embracing uncertainty as the starting point for innovation because the best solutions often come from exploring the unknown.

I am seeking for opportunities in environments that value versatility and creative problem-solving; whether that's working on multiple client projects or building diverse products throughout the week. I am not looking to do the same thing every day; I'm looking to grow through constant exploration and challenges.
